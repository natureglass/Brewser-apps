Three.js r162 — Cubemap (Reflection / Refraction)

Port of webgl_materials_cubemap.html: three Walt heads under a Swedish Royal Castle skybox, each lit by an AmbientLight + central PointLight and rendered with a different cubemap material variant — plain reflection, refraction (CubeRefractionMapping), and reflection mixed with diffuse colour (MixOperation). First milestone that exercises runtime cubemap URL loading end-to-end on the new image-to-texture pipeline.


Left stick orbits the camera. Upstream's controls.minPolarAngle=π/4 / maxPolarAngle=π/1.5 keep the camera off the poles; enableZoom and enablePan are disabled so the camera distance stays fixed at 13. The Stats addon (FPS counter) is dropped — nx.js has no DOM, and the status canvas above already surfaces FPS plus richer diagnostics. CubeTextureLoader is replaced by manual Image + CubeTexture.images[] assignment for the same reason the OBJ port replaced TextureLoader: nx.js's Image bypasses the global fetch, so URLs must be sdmc:/ rather than brewser:// (see nxjs-image-bypasses-global-fetch memory).
