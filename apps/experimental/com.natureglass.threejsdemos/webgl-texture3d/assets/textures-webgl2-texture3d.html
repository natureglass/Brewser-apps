Conformance: textures/webgl2-texture3d

Hand-rolled to pin the 3D-texture surface the milestone exercises.

  • TEXTURE_3D + UNPACK_ALIGNMENT + R32F + RED + FLOAT constants exposed on the WebGL 2 proto
  • bindTexture(TEXTURE_3D, tex) + querying the binding via getParameter(TEXTURE_BINDING_3D)
  • texParameteri(TEXTURE_3D, MIN/MAG_FILTER/WRAP_*) accepts the 3D target
  • texImage3D with both common combos: (RGBA, RGBA, UNSIGNED_BYTE) and (R32F, RED, FLOAT)
  • Full round-trip via passthrough: GLSL ES 3.00 fragment shader declares uniform sampler3D u_data;, samples a known 2×2×2 R32F volume with literal coords, draws to a 1×1 FBO, readback verifies each of 8 texel values
