Three.js r162 — Interactive Cubes

Port of webgl_interactive_cubes.html: a swarm of randomly-colored, randomly-rotated, randomly-scaled BoxGeometry meshes lit by a single DirectionalLight. A Raycaster casts from the right-stick-driven pointer through the scene and highlights the first-intersected cube by setting its material.emissive = red.


Conformance: uniforms/emissive-uniform — pins getUniformLocation("emissive") recognition (the new name added this milestone) plus the setter round-trip via uniform3f/uniform3fv, and verifies sibling lighting uniforms (specular, shininess, diffuse, ambientLightColor) still resolve.

Move the right analog stick to steer the pointer. The hovered cube glows red via material.emissive — milestone #13 forced bridge emissive uniform support in both bridge_color_program and bridge_texture_program. Cube count reduced from upstream's 2000 to 200 for playable FPS on the bridge's per-mesh dispatch overhead.
