Khronos conformance: uniforms/gl-uniformmatrix4fv

Ported from WebGL/sdk/tests/conformance/uniforms/gl-uniformmatrix4fv.html. Verifies that uniformMatrix2fv / uniformMatrix3fv / uniformMatrix4fv reject undersized + non-multiple-sized arrays with INVALID_VALUE, accept correctly-sized data from both TypedArrays AND plain JS Arrays, and (per WebGL 1) reject transpose=true. This is the direct regression test for the 10 new uniform setters and the JS-Array-input fix landed during milestone #5 (misc-controls-orbit).
