Three.js r162 — Orbit Controls

Port of three-r162/examples/misc_controls_orbit.html: 120 cones on a flat plane (upstream uses 500; reduced for bridge per-draw overhead), two directional lights + ambient, exponential fog. First interactive demo. OrbitControls replaced with a gamepad-driven equivalent in libs/orbit-controls.js: Left stick orbit · Right stick pan · ZL/ZR zoom.


Conformance: uniforms/gl-uniformmatrix4fv — pins the 10 new uniform setters this milestone added (uniformMatrix2/3fv, uniform1/2/4fv, uniform2/3/4i/iv) plus the JS-Array-input fix for state.ambient.

A field of 120 cones lit by two directional lights (white key + dim blue fill) plus a gray ambient. Exponential fog fades distant cones toward the gray background. Tilt the left stick to orbit the camera around the scene center, the right stick to pan, and hold ZL/ZR to zoom in/out. Camera distance is clamped between 100 and 500.
