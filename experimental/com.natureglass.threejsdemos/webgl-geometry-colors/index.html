Three.js r162 — Geometry Vertex Colors

Port of three-r162/examples/webgl_geometry_colors.html: three lit IcosahedronGeometry meshes with per-vertex HSL/RGB colors under a single DirectionalLight. Each has a black wireframe overlay and a radial-gradient floor shadow. First milestone to exercise the bridge's directional lighting path (a_normal + u_lightDirection + u_lightColor in bridge_color_program). Tilt the left analog stick to pan the camera.


Conformance: programs/gl-get-active-attribute — verifies getActiveAttrib reports the right name, type, and size across every GLSL attribute type (the bridge added a_normal for this milestone's lighting path).

Three lit icosahedra with per-vertex HSL/RGB coloring on a white background. The bridge applies Lambert diffuse shading using view-space normals from the geometry — faces pointing toward the light (at (0, 0, 1)) are brighter. Each icosahedron has a black wireframe overlay and a radial-gradient floor shadow.
